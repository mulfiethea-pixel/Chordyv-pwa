Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 August 2025

ChordyV (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a music/chord editor delivered as a Progressive Web App (PWA). This policy explains what we collect and how we handle it.

Data we collect

  • Google account basics: when you sign in with Google, we receive your display name and email address from Firebase Authentication.
  • Chord data: all chord content is stored locally on your device. When you use Export/Import, the data is handled as a JSON file that you save and manage.
  • Technical basics: PWA caching (via a service worker) so the app can work offline. We do not use analytics, ad trackers, or third‑party cookies.

Why we process data

  • Authentication (Google Sign‑In) and session personalization.
  • Core app operations (storing, viewing, exporting, and importing chord data).

Where data is stored

  • Account data: handled by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC).
  • Your chord data: stored on your device only, unless you export/share it yourself.

Third parties

We use Firebase Authentication solely for sign‑in. Your account data is processed under Google’s terms and privacy policy.

Security

We rely on Google’s authentication security. We do not host your chord content on our servers. Please keep your device and exported JSON files protected.

Your choices

You may sign out at any time. To manage or delete your Google account data, use your Google Account settings. Chord data can be deleted within the app or by removing exported JSON files.

Children

This app is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will post the new version with the “Last updated” date above.
